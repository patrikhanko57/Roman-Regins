About Roman Reigns

Roman Reigns (Leati Joseph Anoaʻi) is one of the biggest superstars in WWE history. He rose to fame as part of The Shield before becoming the leader of The Bloodline. As "The Tribal Chief", he dominated WWE with one of the longest championship reigns ever.
